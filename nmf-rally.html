A Fully Curated VIP Rally Weekend

One Unforgettable California Rally Experience

August 8, 2026 · San Luis Obispo to Monterey, California

All cars welcome. Think this is only for supercars? Think again — this rally is for everyone, and every car. A fully curated VIP experience: luxury hotel stay, organized route planning, exclusive stops, private dining, rally branding, curated hospitality, and a Monterey finale built around the energy of Car Week.

NMF Rally California '26 is an elevated, fully curated rally experience designed for those who expect more from the road. The weekend begins Saturday at the Porsche of San Luis Obispo for the rally Sticker Party and NMF Cars & Caffeine — guests connect, prepare their vehicles, and kick off the rally in true NMF fashion.

The rally heads north to Morro Bay for a drive down the Embarcadero and a photo stop at the Rock, then on to Paso Robles for a private lunch at The Anderson Paso, a castle-style winery just off Highway 46 West. From there it transitions into one of the most iconic coastal drives in the world — up Highway 1 through Big Sur, with stops at McWay Falls and the legendary Bixby Creek Bridge — concluding in Monterey with a one-night stay at the Monterey Plaza Hotel & Spa and an elegant gala finale: a reception hour, plated dinner, and a charity auction benefiting Along Comes Hope, followed by music and dancing.

Saturday

Sticker Party + NMF Cars & Caffeine — Porsche San Luis Obispo

The rally begins Saturday morning at NMF Cars & Caffeine, hosted at Porsche San Luis Obispo. Drivers check in, rally vehicle graphics are professionally applied, and participants connect before rolling out. This portion is open to the public, so expect a strong turnout of local enthusiasts to send the rally off in true NMF fashion.

The Drive

Morro Bay, Paso Robles, Big Sur & the Monterey Gala

From San Luis Obispo the rally rolls north — a cruise down the Embarcadero in Morro Bay with a photo op at the Rock, then a private VIP lunch at The Anderson Paso, a castle-style winery in Paso Robles. From there, Highway 1 delivers one of the world's most iconic coastal drives — Cambria, Big Sur, McWay Falls, and Bixby Creek Bridge — before arriving in Monterey. The evening closes at the Monterey Plaza Hotel & Spa with cocktail hour, a plated dinner, a charity auction benefiting Along Comes Hope, music, and dancing.

Car Week

Monterey Car Week Begins

Wake up in Monterey and enjoy the energy of Car Week at your own pace — exploring local events, relaxing by the coast, or spending the day with the automotive community. NMF Rally California '26 places you right where the action is.
