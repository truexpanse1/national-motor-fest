A Fully Curated VIP Rally Weekend

One Unforgettable California Rally Experience

August 7–9, 2026

Orange County to Monterey, California — luxury hotel stays, organized route planning, exclusive stops, private dining, rally branding, curated hospitality, and a Monterey finale built around the energy of Car Week.

NMF Rally California '26 is an elevated, invitation-style rally experience built for drivers who want more than just a road trip. This fully curated weekend takes participants from Costa Mesa to Paso Robles, through Big Sur, and into Monterey during one of the most exciting automotive weeks of the year.

The experience includes a rooftop sticker party, luxury hotel stays, a banquet-style driver's breakfast, an organized rally route, a private castle lunch at The Anderson Paso, iconic Highway 1 coastal driving, McWay Falls, Bixby Bridge, and a finale celebration at the Monterey Plaza Hotel & Spa.

Day 1

Rooftop Sticker Party + Hotel Stay

The weekend begins Friday in Costa Mesa with an exclusive rooftop sticker party at Avenue of the Arts. Drivers check in, vehicle graphics are professionally applied, connect with other participants, and kick off the rally in true NMF fashion — followed by a one-night stay before Saturday's driver meeting and departure.

Day 2

Paso Robles, Big Sur & Monterey Gala

Saturday opens with a banquet-style breakfast and driver's meeting before the rally heads north — organized fuel stops in Santa Barbara and Atascadero, then a private VIP lunch at The Anderson Paso castle winery. From there, Highway 1 delivers Big Sur, McWay Falls, and Bixby Creek Bridge before Monterey. The evening closes at the Monterey Plaza Hotel & Spa with cocktail hour, a plated dinner, a charity auction, music, and dancing.

Day 3

Monterey Car Week Begins

Wake up in Monterey and enjoy the energy of Car Week at your own pace — exploring local events, relaxing by the coast, or spending the day with the automotive community. NMF Rally California '26 places you right where the action is.
