One Massive Day of Horsepower

National Motor Fest 3 and the NMF Smoke Show are merging for one massive day of horsepower, tire smoke, custom vehicles, and live competitions. Rather than hosting two separate events, we are bringing the complete National Motor Fest experience to Santa Margarita Ranch on October 10, 2026.

This is not just a burnout competition. This is not just a car show. This is a full-scale motorsports festival built for drivers, builders, families, creators, automotive brands, and fans who live for engines.

From wild burnout machines and high-horsepower race cars to supercars, classics, lowriders, lifted trucks, Jeeps, motorcycles, and imports — if it has an engine, it belongs here.
