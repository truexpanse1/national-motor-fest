NMF3 is not just a car show. It is a full-scale motor festival bringing together every side of the automotive and motorsports world in one unforgettable day.

Expect supercars, hot rods, classics, lifted trucks, Jeeps, off-road builds, lowriders, motorcycles, RVs, specialty vehicles, airplanes, helicopters, vendors, food trucks, music, family fun, and live-action competitions all happening in one place.

From dyno pulls and exhaust battles to hydraulic hopping, sound-off competitions, flex tests, and off-road action, NMF3 is built to keep the crowd moving all day long.

If it has an engine, it belongs here. All Engines. One Movement.

What to Expect at NMF3

Massive Vehicle Show

Supercars, exotics, classics, hot rods, lowriders, lifted trucks, Jeeps, off-road builds, motorcycles, RVs, trailers, specialty vehicles, aircraft, helicopters, and more.

Live Action Events

Dyno pulls, exhaust battles, sound-off competition, hydraulic hopping, flex test, off-road obstacle course, and crowd-drawing demonstrations throughout the day.

Festival Experience

Food trucks, vendors, beer and wine booths, live music, DJ, bounce houses, family zone, and a full day of entertainment.

Historic Location

NMF3 takes place at the Estrella Warbirds Museum in Paso Robles, surrounded by aircraft, military history, and one of the most unique event backdrops in California.
